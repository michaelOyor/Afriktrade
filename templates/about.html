Lawrence Appolos Oyor, Esq. – Managing Director & Chief Executive Officer, AFRIKTRADE Limited

Lawrence Appolos Oyor is the Managing Director & Chief Executive Officer of AFRIKTRADE Limited, a fast-rising e-commerce and cross-border trade solutions company redefining how Africa connects with global markets. With several years experience in corporate and commercial law, regulatory compliance, and business advisory, he brings a rare blend of legal expertise, entrepreneurial acumen, and visionary leadership to the African digital economy. Since 2020, Lawrence has been at the forefront of cross-border payment innovations, informally designing frameworks that simplify transactions between African businesses and international partners. This experience laid the groundwork for AFRIKTRADE’s mission to deliver seamless cross-border payments, e-commerce platforms, and integrated logistics solutions that empower African businesses to source directly globally. A dynamic leader, public speaker, mentor, and Pan-Africanist, Lawrence is also the founder of the African Young Professionals Network, where he champions leadership, innovation, and economic empowerment for Africa’s next generation. His work reflects a commitment to leveraging fintech, trade logistics, and digital commerce to position Africa as a competitive force in the global digital marketplace. Under his leadership, AFRIKTRADE is not just an e-commerce company but a strategic growth partner for African entrepreneurs, providing the tools, platforms, and payment infrastructure to unlock new markets and opportunities. ]

Emmanuel Obare Chief Operating Officer, AFRIKTRADE Limited

Emmanuel Obare is the Chief Operating Officer of AFRIKTRADE Limited, where he spearheads operations with a focus on cross-border payment innovation, digital infrastructure, and scalable trade solutions. A seasoned technologist with global experience across Canada, the UK, the Middle East, and Africa, Emmanuel has built and deployed cutting-edge fintech platforms that power seamless international commerce. At AFRIKTRADE, he leads the development of our payment and logistics ecosystem, enabling businesses and consumers in Africa to trade effortlessly with partners across the globe. His expertise spans blockchain-based financial services, peer-to-peer transaction systems, and enterprise-grade e-commerce platforms, aligning directly with AFRIKTRADE’s mission to simplify trade and expand market access across continents. Renowned for turning complex technologies into practical solutions, Emmanuel has engineered platforms that boosted adoption by over 30%, optimized cross-border transactions, and enhanced user trust through secure and transparent systems. His leadership ensures AFRIKTRADE delivers on its vision of becoming Africa’s most trusted gateway to global trade. With a First-Class Honours degree in Computing from the University of East London, Emmanuel blends technical mastery with operational excellence, driving AFRIKTRADE’s commitment to innovation, reliability, and growth in Africa’s e-commerce landscape.

Collins Nteptep Director of Legal and Regulatory Compliance - AFRIKTRADE Limited.

About Afriktrade Limited

Our Company

Afriktrade Limited is an emerging e-commerce and trade facilitation company headquartered in Abuja, Nigeria. Incorporated on the 23rd day of May, 2025. Our mission is to bridge the trade gap between Africa and other continents by creating a reliable, integrated platform that empowers businesses and consumers to access global opportunities effortlessly. We specialize in cross-border trade solutions, combining secure payment systems, global sourcing, and end-to-end logistics to deliver a seamless international trading experience. By partnering with strategic global enterprises, we aim to position Africa at the forefront of the digital trade revolution.

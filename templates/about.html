Lawrence Appolos Oyor, Esq. – Managing Director & Chief Executive Officer

Lawrence Appolos Oyor, Managing Director & CEO of AFRIKTRADE Limited, leads a fast-growing e-commerce and cross-border trade solutions company connecting Africa to global markets. With expertise in corporate law, compliance, and business advisory, he drives AFRIKTRADE’s mission to provide seamless cross-border payments, e-commerce platforms, and logistics solutions. Since 2020, he has pioneered payment innovations for African businesses. A Pan-Africanist, Lawrence founded the African Young Professionals Network, promoting leadership and economic empowerment. Under his leadership, AFRIKTRADE empowers African entrepreneurs with tools and infrastructure to access global markets.

Emmanuel Obare – Chief Operating Officer

Emmanuel Obare, Chief Operating Officer of AFRIKTRADE Limited, oversees operations, focusing on cross-border payment innovation, digital infrastructure, and scalable trade solutions. A seasoned technologist with global experience in Canada, the UK, the Middle East, and Africa, he develops fintech platforms for seamless international commerce. At AFRIKTRADE, Emmanuel leads the creation of payment and logistics ecosystems, leveraging blockchain, peer-to-peer systems, and e-commerce platforms to simplify trade. His solutions have increased adoption by over 30% and enhanced transaction security. Holding a First-Class Honours degree in Computing from the University of East London, he drives AFRIKTRADE’s vision as Africa’s trusted gateway to global trade.

Collins Nteptep – Director of Legal and Regulatory Compliance

Collins Nteptep leads Legal and Regulatory Compliance at AFRIKTRADE Limited, ensuring the company operates with the highest standards of governance, risk management, and international trade compliance. With a Master’s degree in Intellectual Property Law, specializing in Trademark Law, he combines deep legal expertise with strategic foresight to protect AFRIKTRADE’s brand and innovation in the global marketplace. As a Barrister and Solicitor of the Supreme Court of Nigeria, Collins plays a pivotal role in shaping AFRIKTRADE’s compliance culture, driving trust and transparency in its cross-border e-commerce, payments, and logistics operations. Recognized for his forward-looking approach, he champions regulatory innovation that positions AFRIKTRADE at the forefront of Africa–International digital trade.

Hon. Michael Oyor – Director of People, Government and Public Affairs

Hon. Michael Oyor is the Director of People, Government and Public Affairs at AFRIKTRADE Limited. He brings a unique blend of scientific training and human resource expertise, holding degrees in Applied & Environmental Biology and Employment Relations & Human Resource Management from Rivers State University. In his role, he provides strategic leadership in government relations, public affairs, and organizational people management. He drives AFRIKTRADE’s engagement with government agencies, policymakers, and key stakeholders, while also shaping a people-first culture that supports the company’s growth and long-term sustainability. With a reputation for integrity, effective communication, and relationship building, Hon. Oyor plays a critical role in positioning AFRIKTRADE as a trusted partner in Africa’s evolving e-commerce and cross-border trade landscape.
